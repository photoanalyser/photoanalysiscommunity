Snigdha Kar is the founder of the famous Kolkata based photography club PLAAPA (Photography Lovers an Abode of Photo Addicts). Before creating the PhotoAnalyser , she was the Director of Photography of numerous Photography workshops where she oversaw the photographic vision of budding photographers to out weigh their potential . Prior to that she also has been appointed as the chairperson by Federation of Indian Photography's first all women juror circuit. She also have been Awarded more than 50 times by various International and National Photography Salons and also have 600+ accepted works. Right now she is also working as a juror in various Photography Salons across India . She recieved AFIAP in 2019.
